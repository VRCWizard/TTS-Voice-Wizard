## Features
Use TTS Voice Wizard's accessibility features to improve your VRChat experience (**it works outside of VRChat too!**)<br />

üéôÔ∏è You can convert your **Speech-to-Text** and back to Speech through various Speech Recognition and **Text-to-Speech methods** <br />

üí¨ You can send what you say as OSC messages to VRChat to be displayed on your avatar using **[KillFrenzyAvatarText](https://github.com/killfrenzy96/KillFrenzyAvatarText) or VRChats Chatbox** <br />

üåê The app can **translate** your speech from one language to over **50 other supported languages** <br />

üîä There are **100+ different voices** with various customization options so you can pick a voice that best suits you <br />

üéµ Display the **current song** you are listening to on Spotify or via your browser <br />

üîã Display **tracker and controller battery life** in conjunction with [XSOverlay](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1173510/XSOverlay/) <br />

‚ù§Ô∏è Use in conjuntion with [Pulsoid](https://pulsoid.net/) or [HRtoVRChat_OSC](https://github.com/200Tigersbloxed/HRtoVRChat_OSC) to enable you to display your **heartrate** in VRChat's Chatbox <br />

üó£Ô∏è Control VRChat avatar parameters with **voice commands**

ü´µ Display customizable and **interactive counters** for amount of times a VRChat contact receiver has been touched

üìπ For a more detailed look at the app's features, check out the [Demonstration Video](https://youtu.be/wBRUcx9EWes) (although it's a bit outdated)
